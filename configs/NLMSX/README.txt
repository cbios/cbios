C-BIOS machine configurations for NLMSX
=========================================

Installation instructions:
1. Copy "C-BIOS - MSX", "C-BIOS - MSX2" and "C-BIOS - MSX2" subdirectories to
   "Config\_Default" in the NLMSX installation directory.
2. Copy "cbios_main_msx*.rom", "cbios_sub.rom" and "cbios_music.rom" into the
   "ROMS" directory.
