C-BIOS machine configurations for blueMSX
=========================================

Installation instructions:
1. Copy "MSX - C-BIOS", "MSX2 - C-BIOS" and "MSX2+ - C-BIOS" subdirectories
   to "Machines" in the blueMSX installation directory.
2. Copy "cbios_main_msx1.rom" into the "MSX - C-BIOS", "cbios_main_msx2.rom"
   and "cbios_sub.rom" in "MSX2 - C-BIOS" and "cbios_main_msx2+.rom",
   "cbios_sub.rom" and "cbios_music.rom" in "MSX2+ - C-BIOS" directories.
